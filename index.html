Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to NoteScience! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web platform. Please read this privacy policy carefully.

By using NoteScience, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with the data practices described in this policy, you should not use our application.

Information We Collect

NoteScience collects the following types of information:

Personal Information

When you create an account with NoteScience, we collect:

  • Your full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Password (encrypted)

Educational Information

To provide personalized educational content, we may collect:

  • Your educational level (Class 9th or 10th)
  • Subjects you're studying
  • Notes and study materials you create or access

Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain information when you use our app, including:

  • Device information (type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • IP address
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash reports and performance data

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in the following ways:

  • To create and maintain your account
  • To provide personalized educational content for Class 9th and 10th students
  • To improve our app and user experience
  • To communicate with you about app updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • To analyze app usage and trends
  • To ensure the security of our application

Advertising

NoteScience uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use information about you to provide personalized ads. This information may include:

  • Your device's advertising identifier
  • IP address
  • App usage data
  • General location information

Note: We do not share your personal information (name, email, phone number) with advertisers. The advertising information collected by AdMob is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

You can learn more about how Google uses data when you use our app by visiting: How Google uses information from sites or apps that use their services.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

If you wish to delete your account and associated data, you can do so through the app settings or by contacting us directly at developerahmed243@gmail.com.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • The right to access the personal information we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information
  • The right to object to processing of your personal information
  • The right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section.

International Data Transfers

NoteScience is operated from Pakistan. If you are located outside Pakistan, please be aware that any information you provide to us may be transferred to and processed in Pakistan. By using our app and providing your information, you consent to this transfer.

Children's Privacy

NoteScience is designed for students in Class 9th and 10th, who are typically minors. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent
  • We encourage parents and guardians to monitor their children's use of our app
  • If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information
